Fine print

Booking terms

Liability at owner's risk

  • Documents are covered up to ₹1,500 per consignment.
  • Non-document parcels are covered up to ₹2,500 per consignment.
  • All shipments are booked at owner's risk beyond the declared liability limit.
  • Higher-value shipments can be insured separately at the counter — ask us before booking.
Standard terms apply per DTDC / Rivigo network policy. Confirm current rates and coverage at the counter.
